Tangbin?

Hello! This is Tangbin Chen. I sometimes also go by Arron (a name I like but is not used much lately).

![[By the black sea.jpg]]

I was an engineering student who had always been interested in social questions. Very luckily, I got admitted to a master program in Computational Social Science at Linköping University where I met a lot on interesting people and received my desired training.

After my graduation in 2022, I decided to continue studying as a doctoral student and worked at the Institute for Analytical Sociology at Linköping University for another year. After that, I was offered a PhD position at Karolinska Institutet to do social epidemiological research. I am very looking forward to my coming journey~

My main interest is in social influence and network study. My doctoral research will focus on health-related behaviors, especially their diffusion through social networks and network-based intervention.

Hobbies?
